> ## Audit logs are opinions. Protocol C is cryptographic proof.

Your cloud provider's audit log is an editable text file. AetherCloud turns your
audit trail from opinion into a cryptographically signed chain of evidence — and
runs a fleet of AI agents on top of it.

## AetherCloud is a desktop-native, agentic AI platform. Five pillars:

### 🔐 Trust & provenance — *the moat*
Every file operation and every AI output is SHA-256 hashed, signed with a
quantum-seeded ephemeral ECDSA key destroyed after a single use, RFC 3161
timestamped, and appended to an immutable log — exportable as an evidence-grade
proof package. Tampered AI responses are mathematically rejected: the signature
fails and the system refuses to proceed. *Protocol C.*

### 🧠 Self-improvement — *QOPC*
The Quantum Optimized Prompt Circuit watches what you accept, revise, publish, or
discard, and tunes its own prompt weights. Your AI gets measurably better the
more you use it — no config, no fine-tuning.

### 🛰 Orchestration — *NEO + KRONUS*
Don't just prompt one model — command a fleet. NEO and KRONUS triage your task
and route it to the right model automatically, gated to your plan and balance.

### 🗄 Memory & Vault
A sealed Vault gives your agents real, persistent memory of your files — with the
full chain of custody attached. Your files never touch a third-party cloud; you
control the infrastructure, and every model call is signed.

### 🎬 Creative & app-building
Go concept → campaign without switching tabs: multi-agent workflows draft, build,
and drop verified results into your Vault. Build a workflow once, set it on a
daily schedule in plain English, and walk away — the agents run while you sleep.
