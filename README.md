## Why people switch

A chat window gives you one model, one message at a time, and a transcript it
forgets tomorrow. AetherCloud gives you:

- **Agents that finish tasks**, not just answer questions — they read your files,
  build the thing, and drop verified results back into your Vault.
- **A Vault with real memory** that every agent shares — and that travels with you
  to your Aether AI on the web.
- **Workflows you build once and forget** — set them on a schedule in plain English
  and the agents run on their own.
- **Proof, not vibes** — every file op and every AI output is signed into a tamper-
  evident chain you can export.

## What you can do

### 🗂 Projects + the Agent IDE
Open a project, point the agents at it, and work side-by-side with them in a
built-in IDE. Browse files, run multi-step builds, review diffs, and let agents
edit alongside you — all on one surface. No tab-switching, no copy-paste between a
chat tab and your editor.

### ⚙️ Workflows
Chain agents into a repeatable pipeline — draft, build, verify, file the result —
then **build it once and set it on a daily schedule in plain English.** Walk away;
the workflow runs while you sleep and lands the output in your Vault.

### 🗄 The Vault — your AI's living memory
A sealed Vault gives your agents real, persistent context: your files, your past
work, your decisions. It's not a dumb folder — it's the active working memory the
agents reason from. And it **syncs to your Aether AI on the web**, so you can
manage the same Vault and pick up the same context from your browser. Your files
never touch a third-party cloud; you control the infra, and every model call is
signed.

### 🛰 Orchestration — NEO + KRONUS
Don't pick a model — command a fleet. NEO and KRONUS triage your task and route it
to the right model automatically, gated to your plan and balance.

### 🔐 Trust & provenance — the moat
Every file operation and every AI output is SHA-256 hashed, signed with a
quantum-seeded ephemeral ECDSA key destroyed after a single use, RFC 3161
timestamped, and appended to an immutable log — exportable as an evidence-grade
proof package. Tampered AI responses are mathematically rejected: the signature
fails and the system refuses to proceed. *Protocol C.*

### 🧠 Self-improvement — QOPC
The Quantum Optimized Prompt Circuit watches what you accept, revise, publish, or
discard, and tunes its own prompt weights. Your AI gets measurably better the more
you use it — no config, no fine-tuning.
